Trainer

Hello. I am Kana and I train people in London.

While I was working at the gym, I noticed that even though so many people have a great motivation towards exercise, they actually don’t know which equipment to use and the proper way to exercise to attain their goals.

I would like to help people to make their exercise more effective.

I am happy to help anybody with different goals and fitness levels. It could be training for certain kind of sports, improving your own posture, losing weight, gaining muscles or simply becoming healthy.

I want my clients to realize that with just a little bit of change in your everyday life, you can make such a big change in your life. I would like you to enjoy a healthier life without too much constraint. It is wonderful to have a healthy life style!

I will make your session very enjoyable every time. Please don’t hesitate asking me questions by sending a message, I would be glad to answer.
